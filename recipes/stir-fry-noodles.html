Ghanaian stir fry noddles

Ghanaian Stir-Fry noodles are a quick, flavorful street-style noodle dish where instant Indomie noodles are boiled,

then tossed in a hot pan with onions, tomatoes, peppers, and often extras like egg, sardine,

or sausage. It’s spicy, colorful, and loved as a fast meal or late-night snack across Ghana

Ingredients for making stir fry noodles

  • 2 packs of Indomie noodles
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • 1 fresh pepper (optional, for heat)
  • 1 egg (optional, for protein)
  • 2 tbsp oil (vegetable oi)
  • The Indomie spice sachets (that come inside the pack)
